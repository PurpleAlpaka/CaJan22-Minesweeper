The rules are as follows:
IMPORTANT: Start the game with a left click

Controls:
Left Click: open a cell and show it's contents
Right Click: Mark a cell with a flag
R: Restart
Escape: Close Modals

The goal of the game is to uncover all the squares that do not contain mines, and mark all mines with a flag
without being "blown up" by clicking on a square with a mine underneath.

If you click on an empty cell, it will show how many mines are neighbouring that cell, if the number is 0 it will show an empty cell and expand it's neighbours.
If you click on a cell containing a mine and have lives left (shown in the red hearts above the board), that cell will flash red, indicating you clicked on a mine, and your life count will reduce by one.
When you use all 3 lives, you lose the game. There are certain helps and cheats available for you, if you so choose. the helps are:
If you click one of the lit up lamps above the board, your next left click will become a hint click, showing a cell and it's immediate neighbours for a second, then hiding them again, without affecting lives.
If you click on the Safe Clicks button, a random safe cell (a cell not containing a mine) will flash in green for a few seconds.
If you click on the undo button, it will take the board state back one click, until the start of the game.
Difficulties:
You can only have one of these active at a time.
Easy: A 4 by 4 board, with 2 mines
Medium: A 8 by 8 board, with 12 mines
Hard: A 12 by 12 board, with 30 mines
Game modes:
You can have only one of these active at a time, or none (A difficulty will always be active).
Manual - Place your own mines (You have to place EXACTLY as much mines as the difficulty you are playing on requires).
7 BOOM - Mines will be placed on cells who are a multiplication of 7.
